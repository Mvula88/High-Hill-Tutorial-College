About Us

Excellence in Education Since Day One

High-Hill Tutorial Centre is a fully accredited educational institution registered with the Ministry of Education, Innovation, Youth, Sports, Arts and Culture as both a Tuition and Examination Centre (Reg No.: CC/2023/02218).

We specialize in providing quality part-time tuition and NSSCO examination preparation for students in Grade 10, 11, and 12. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to helping every student achieve their academic goals.
